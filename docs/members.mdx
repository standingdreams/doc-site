To enable Members/Subscriptions, visit the Labs page of your admin. Click on the "Members" button to get to the Members settings. Clicking "Enable members" toggle will enable members and show new options.

From the Members area, you can set Subscription pricing, connect you site to Stripe, and many other features. For more information and instructions, please visit Ghost's documentation on [Members](https://ghost.org/help/setup-members/).
